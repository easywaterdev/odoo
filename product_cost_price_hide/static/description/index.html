Product Cost Price Hide/Show

This module add feature to hide a product cost price.



Main Features:
  • Add a 'Cost Price Manager' group.
  • Hide a cost price on 'product' page.
  • Hide a cost price on 'product variants' page.
  • Hide a cost price on 'Change Standard Price' wizard.
